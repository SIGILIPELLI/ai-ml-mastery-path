# Level 1 · Entry <span class="level-badge">Foundations</span>

Goal: get comfortable with the scientific Python stack, train and honestly
evaluate classical ML models with scikit-learn, understand the fundamentals
of neural networks with PyTorch, and ship a complete end-to-end tabular ML
project.

## Modules

1. [Setup & the Scientific Python Stack](01-setup-scientific-python.md)
2. [NumPy & Pandas Essentials](02-numpy-pandas.md)
3. [Data Preparation](03-data-preparation.md)
4. [Regression](04-regression.md)
5. [Classification](05-classification.md)
6. [Clustering & Unsupervised Learning](06-clustering-unsupervised.md)
7. [Model Evaluation & Cross-Validation](07-model-evaluation.md)
8. [Feature Engineering & Pipelines](08-feature-engineering-pipelines.md)
9. [Neural Network Fundamentals with PyTorch](09-neural-networks-pytorch.md)
10. [Capstone — End-to-End Tabular ML Project](10-capstone-project.md)

By the end of this level you'll be able to take a raw tabular dataset,
prepare it with a leakage-proof pipeline, train and compare several models,
evaluate them with cross-validation, and save the winner for reuse — plus
train your first neural network from scratch in PyTorch.
